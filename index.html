About us

Opened in early 2019, it’s a new venture for us in sharing our love for good food. We rather liked the old owners’ name for the place and so kept it as Down to Earth Glasgow.

Most of our dishes are vegan and there’s no beef or dairy in the kitchen. We have cow’s milk for those who like it with their tea though and locally sourced chicken and pork dishes. Our different vegan burgers and fish tacos seem to be flying out the door! All made fresh daily by our head chef Kevin and his lovely, food obsessed team.

As much of our ingredients as possible are from local suppliers, many who are start-ups like ourselves. Our fresh bread, burgers and some cakes are made by friends like @DaringBurgers.

Cold pressed juices are a big part of who we are here at Down to Earth. We juice a range of different flavours every morning and what’s available changes seasonally. The perfect kick start, the hangover cure, the perk you up….we’re loving the variety.

We’re thinking cold pressed cocktails could be our next thing to try.
